# Freddie Mac Single-Family Loan-Level Dataset — File Layout & Data Dictionary

This document covers the layout of each **Origination Data File** and **Monthly Performance Data File**, including every data element, its valid values/calculations, data type, and length.

A SAS script to parse and combine the dataset files is available on the Single-Family Loan-Level Dataset website.

## How to Read This Dictionary

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Column Position** | Position of the column in the disclosure file. |
| **Formal Name and Definition** | Name and definition of the loan-level data element. |
| **Valid Values/Calculations** | Allowable values for the specific data field and the calculations used (if applicable). |
| **Type (Data Type)** | The type of data found in each column: <br>• **Alpha** – contains only letters <br>• **Alpha-numeric** – contains letters and numbers <br>• **Numeric** – contains only numbers <br>• **Date** – represents a specific date (Y = Year, M = Month). Example: YYYYMM (201207) = July 2012 |
| **Length** | Represents the maximum number of characters allowed for the data field. |

> Note: The header record row has been removed from all files in the Dataset and sample dataset; the position of columns in each file remains unchanged.

| 14 | **CHANNEL** | Indicates whether a Broker or Correspondent originated or was involved in the origination. ## Part 2: Monthly Performance Data File

| Col # | Field Name | Definition | Valid Values / Calculations | Type | Length |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **LOAN SEQUENCE NUMBER** | Unique identifier assigned to each loan. | PYYQnXXXXXXX <br>• Product F = FRM, A = ARM <br>• YYQn = origination year and quarter <br>• XXXXXXX = randomly assigned digits | Alpha-numeric | 12 |
| 2 | **MONTHLY REPORTING PERIOD** | The as-of month for the loan information contained in the loan record. | • YYYYMM | Date | 6 |
| 3 | **CURRENT ACTUAL UPB** | Reflects the mortgage ending balance as reported by the servicer for the corresponding period. For fixed-rate mortgages, derived from servicer-reported balance, including scheduled/unscheduled principal reductions. For loans with modifications or payment deferrals, may include non-interest-bearing "deferred" amounts; equals sum of Current Interest-Bearing UPB + Current Non-Interest Bearing UPB. If > $500, rounded to nearest $1,000 for the first six payment periods; not rounded after a modification. | Calculation: (interest bearing UPB) + (non-interest bearing UPB) | Numeric Literal Decimal | 12 |
| 4 | **CURRENT LOAN DELINQUENCY STATUS** | A value corresponding to the number of days the borrower is delinquent, based on the Due Date of Last Paid Installment (DDLPI) reported by servicers, calculated under the Mortgage Bankers Association (MBA) method. If a loan has been acquired by REO, the value reflects that status instead of the delinquency day count. | • 0 = Current, or <30 days delinquent <br>• 1 = 30–59 days delinquent <br>• 2 = 60–89 days delinquent <br>• 3 = 90–119 days delinquent <br>• …and so on… <br>• RA = REO Acquisition | Alpha-numeric | 3 |

| 6 | **REMAINING MONTHS TO LEGAL MATURITY** | The remaining number of months to the mortgage maturity date. | 7 | **DEFECT SETTLEMENT DATE** | For underwriting defects: the date of (a) repurchase by the related seller/servicer, (b) a mutually agreed alternative remedy (e.g., indemnification), or (c) Freddie Mac's discretionary waiver of enforcement for an unconfirmed underwriting defect. | 8 | **MODIFICATION FLAG** | A flag indicating if the loan has been modified in the current period or a prior period. | • Y = Current Period Modification <br>• P = Prior Period Modification <br>• Null = Not Modified | Alpha | 1 |
| 9 | **ZERO BALANCE CODE** | A code indicating the reason the loan's balance was reduced to zero. | • 01 = Prepaid or Matured (Voluntary Payoff) <br>• 02 = Third Party Sale <br>• 03 = Short Sale or Charge Off <br>• 09 = REO Disposition <br>• 15 = Whole Loan sales <br>• 16 = Reperforming loan securitizations <br>• 96 = Defect prior to other termination event | Numeric | 2 |
| 10 | **ZERO BALANCE EFFECTIVE DATE** | The date on which the event triggering the Zero Balance Code took place. | • YYYYMM <br>• Space (6) = Not Applicable | Date | 6 |
| 11 | **CURRENT INTEREST RATE** | Reflects the current interest rate on the mortgage note, taking into account any loan modifications. | — | Numeric Literal Decimal | 8 |
| 12 | **CURRENT NON-INTEREST BEARING UPB** | The non-interest-bearing portion of the UPB for a given mortgage. | $ Amount | Numeric | 12 |
| 13 | **DUE DATE OF LAST PAID INSTALLMENT (DDLPI)** | The due date through which the loan's scheduled principal and interest is paid, regardless of when the installment was actually paid. | • YYYYMM | Date | 6 |
| 14 | **MI RECOVERIES** | Mortgage Insurance Recoveries — proceeds received by Freddie Mac in the event of credit losses, based on claims under a mortgage insurance policy. Note: loans with a Defect Settlement Date populated will show 'Not Applicable' (null). | $ Amount | Numeric Literal Decimal | 12 |
| 15 | **NET SALE PROCEEDS** | The amount remitted to Freddie Mac from a property disposition or loan sale (may be an allocated amount for bulk sales) after allowable selling expenses are deducted from gross sales proceeds. | 16 | **NON MI RECOVERIES** | Non-MI Recoveries — proceeds received by Freddie Mac from confirmed defect/make whole proceeds, non-sale income (e.g., tax or insurance refunds), hazard insurance proceeds, rental receipts, positive escrow, and/or other miscellaneous credits. | 17 | **TOTAL EXPENSES** | Allowable expenses Freddie Mac bears in acquiring, maintaining, and/or disposing of a property (excluding selling expenses, which are subtracted from gross sales proceeds for Net Sale Proceeds). | 27 | **ZERO BALANCE REMOVAL UPB** | The amount of total UPB remaining on the loan immediately prior to the application of the Zero Balance Code. | $ Amount | Numeric Literal Decimal | 12 |

| 29 | **DELINQUENCY DUE TO DISASTER** | A flag indicating the Servicer has reported disaster-related hardship as defined by the Freddie Mac Seller/Servicer Guide. | 32 | **INTEREST BEARING UPB** | The current interest-bearing UPB of the modified mortgage. | $ Amount | Numeric Literal Decimal | 12 |

### Quick Reference: Common Special Codes Across This Dictionary

| Code | Common Meaning |
|---|---|
| 9 / 99 / 999 / 9999 | Not Available (the exact number of 9s depends on the field's length) |
| Null / Space | Not Applicable, Unknown, or field doesn't apply to this loan |
| YYYYMM | Date format used throughout (Year + Month, no day) |
| $ Amount | A dollar value field |
